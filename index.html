HOW TO PLAY
  • Tap and hold a colored dot, then drag to its matching pair (or any dot sharing its color) to draw a connecting line.
  • Connect every dot to finish the wave and hear the full song play.
  • Longer, more winding lines score more than short direct ones — the scenic route pays better.
  • A line can't cross a barrier (dashed) or another line. Some barriers rotate, and can snap a connection they sweep through even after it's finished.
  • Pinch or scroll to zoom, and drag empty space to pan around once you're zoomed in.
  • Tap HINT if you're not sure what's still unconnected — every unconnected dot flashes white.
  • Tap the pause button any time to save your progress or adjust settings.
